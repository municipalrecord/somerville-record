Order (ID # 23-0070) Sent for Discussion: By Councilor Wilson and Councilor Pineda Neufeld That the Director of the Office of Sustainability and Environment update this Council on plans for a pilot of on-street direct current fast charging stations for electric vehicles. 2. Order (ID # 23-0092) Sent for Discussion: By Councilor Pineda Neufeld and Councilor Wilson That the Directors of Mobility and Traffic and Parking discuss with this Council the use of meters at electric vehicle charging stations and ability for the City's vendor to impose a monetary charge after a certain amount of time that the vehicle is parked there. 3.

City of Somerville, Massachusetts City Council Confirmation of Appointments and Personnel Matters Committee Meeting Minutes 7:00 PM Wednesday, April 3, 2024 The meeting took place virtually via Zoom and was called to order at 7:00 PM by Chair Pineda Neufeld and adjourned at 8:02 PM on a roll call vote of 5 in favor (Councilors Mbah, Sait, Burnley, McLaughlin and Pineda Neufeld), none against and none absent. Others present: Chief Fire Engineer Charles Breen, SFD Deputy Chief Sean Tierney, Director of Human Resources Anne Gill, Hannah Carrillo – Legislative Liaison, Peter Forcellese – Legislative Clerk. . Confirmation of Appointments and Personnel Matters Committee Meeting Minutes April 3, 2024 Chief. D.C. Silva is an Air Force veteran, a registered nurse, and holds a master’s degree in Public Administration Emergency Services. Councilor Mbah asked the candidate what some of the things are he would change and where he sees the department going and how he would bring it to that next level. D.C. Silva explained that serving in his current position has presented a higher level of challenges. Now, he deals with the variety of development being built in the city and is trying to add technological changes in the fire safety office. Additionally, being a senior officer, he looks forward to taking on more responsibility. Councilor Mbah asked about his leadership style and D.C. Silva said he tries to be a hybrid leader, i.e., not an autocrat, but not a pushover either. He likes to be charismatic and get people to work for him because they want to, not because he told them to. He gave credit to the people in his office for the amount of work that is getting done, rather than to himself. Chair Pineda Neufeld asked what challenges might be ahead for him and the department and D.C. Silva said that it’s important to be competent by keeping up with the latest developments in fire fighting, e.g., lithium ion batteries, and to be calm and when directing fire fighters on scene. He said that there’s a lot of homework to do to keep up as a student of the city in order to be aware of what’s going on. He said that he’s excited about taking on more responsibility. Chair Pineda Neufeld explained that the appointment would not be official until it is approved by the City Council at its next meeting on April 11, 2024. RECOMMENDED TO BE APPROVED RESULT: Chairperson Pineda Neufeld, Ward One City Councilor McLaughlin, City Councilor At Large Burnley Jr., Ward Five City Councilor Sait and Ward One City Councilor Mbah AYE: 10.

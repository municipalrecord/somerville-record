Order (ID # 22-1594) Sent for Discussion: By Councilor Ewen-Campen, Councilor Clingan and Councilor Wilson That the Director of Water and Sewer update this Council on the cause of the sinkhole near 370 Medford Street and what is being done to address this problem. Veterans Services 11. Order (ID # 22-1360) Sent for Discussion: By Councilor McLaughlin An order that the Director of Veterans’ Services update this Council on the status of the veterans’ memorials previously removed due to construction of the new high school.

Public Utilities and Public Works Committee Regular Meeting Agenda November 16, 2022 Order (ID # 22-1692) Sent for Discussion: By Councilor Wilson and Councilor Clingan That the Director of Water and Sewer report to this Council, the account, purchase and usage information requested within. Order (ID # 22-1691) Sent for Discussion: By Councilor Clingan, Councilor Wilson and Councilor McLaughlin That the Director of Water and Sewer appear before this Council to address the issue of constituents reporting unusually high water bill spikes. ISD Order (ID # 22-1796) Sent for Discussion: By Councilor Clingan, Councilor Burnley Jr., Councilor Davis, Councilor Ewen-Campen, Councilor Gomez Mouakad, Councilor Kelly, Councilor McLaughlin, Councilor Pineda Neufeld, Councilor Scott, Councilor Strezo and Councilor Wilson That the Director of Inspectional Services develop a policy requiring all redevelopments to post the information detailed within on the outer perimeter of the property. Fire Order (ID # 22-0193) Sent for Discussion: By Councilor Clingan, Councilor McLaughlin, Councilor Pineda Neufeld, Councilor Strezo and Councilor Gomez Mouakad That the Chief Fire Engineer provide this Council with the plan for staffing the future Assembly Square fire station. Order (ID # 22-0185) Sent for Discussion: By Councilor Davis, Councilor Clingan and Councilor Gomez Mouakad That the Chief Fire Engineer report on the safety risks for all municipal buildings not fully equipped with automatic sprinkler systems. Sustainability Order (ID # 22-1705) Sent for Discussion: By Councilor Wilson That the Director of the Office of Sustainability and Environment update this Council on the status of household hazardous waste collection.

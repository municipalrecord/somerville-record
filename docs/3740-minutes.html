City of Somerville, Massachusetts City Council City Council Organizational Meeting Meeting Minutes 5:00 PM Monday, January 6, 2025 NOTICE: This is NOT the official version of the City Council's minutes. While reasonable efforts have been made to assure the accuracy of the data provided, do not rely on this information without first checking with the City Clerk. 1. OPENING CEREMONIES Councilor Lance Davis, being the oldest member in age, presided and called the meeting to order at 5:05 PM. 1.1. Call of the Roll. Roll Call (ID # 25-0001) Councilor Mbah arrived at 5:11 PM. Councilor Strezo arrived 5:15 PM. PLACED ON FILE RESULT: City Councilor At Large Willie Burnley Jr. City Councilor At Large Wilfred N. Davis Ward Seven City Councilor Judy Pineda Neufeld Present 2. ELECTION OF OFFICERS 2.1. Election of a President for 2025. Election (ID # 25-0002) MOTION: That Councilor Judy Pineda Neufeld be elected President of this City Council. Councilor Pineda Neufeld was nominated by Councilor Wilson, the nomination was seconded by Councilor Ewen-Campen. There being no further nominations, Councilor Ewen-Campen moved to close nominations, the motion was seconded by Councilor Wilson and approved. Councilor Pineda Neufeld was elected as President of the City Council for 2025. City Clerk Kimberly Wells administered the oath of office to Councilor Pineda Neufeld, who then assumed the chair.

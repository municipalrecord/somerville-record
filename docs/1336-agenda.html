City of Somerville, Massachusetts City Council Special Meeting Agenda City Council Chamber and Remote Participation 7:00 PM Thursday, May 30, 2024 Published May 28, 2024, 6:35 PM, Revised Published May 30, 2024, 12:30 PM. This Special Meeting of the City Council has been called by the Mayor solely for the presentation of the proposed FY 2025 municipal and enterprise fund budgets, with related appropriations, transfers, and other financial items. NOTICE: Pursuant to Chapter 2 of the Acts of 2023, this meeting of the City Council may be conducted via remote participation. We will post an audio recording, audiovideo recording, transcript, or other comprehensive record of these proceedings as soon as possible after the meeting on the City of Somerville website and local cable access government channels.

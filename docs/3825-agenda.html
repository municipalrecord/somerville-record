School Building Facilities and Maintenance Special Committee Regular Meeting Agenda April 14, 2025 3. Order (ID # 25-0466) Sent for Discussion: By Councilor Strezo That the Director of Parks and Recreation report on the process by which Ginny Smithers Pool patrons are promptly notified of pool cancellations or schedule changes, including any modifications that could be made to that process in light of the March 2025 temporary pool closure. 4. Order (ID # 25-0642) Sent for Discussion: By Councilor Sait, Councilor Wilson and Councilor McLaughlin That the Director of Parks and Recreation and the Commissioner of Public Works update this Council on the continued closure of Ginny Smithers Sanders Pool and address the lack of communication with users of the pool. 5. Resolution (ID # 25-0727) Sent for Discussion: By Councilor Davis That the Administration report to this Council on the cause of the reported chemical imbalance at the Ginny Smithers Pool and the process being used to address the imbalance. 6. Resolution (ID # 25-0729) Sent for Discussion: By Councilor Davis That the Administration develop a new process to address ongoing maintenance at the Ginny Smithers Pool and report to this Council to describe that process. 7. Order (ID # 25-0602) Sent for Discussion: By Councilor Pineda Neufeld That the Commissioner of Public Works provide an update and timeline for repairs to damaged play structures at the Capuano Early Education Center schoolyard. 8. Order (ID # 25-0603) Sent for Discussion: By Councilor Pineda Neufeld That the Commissioner of Public Works plant natural turf in the muddy greenspace area at the Capuano Early Education Center in advance of the May 3 Family Fun Day celebration for the Capuano’s 20th anniversary.

Land Use Committee Regular Meeting Agenda September 4, 2025 4. Order (ID # 25-0338) Sent for Discussion: By Councilor McLaughlin That the Director of Planning, Preservation and Zoning provide the Committee on Land Use with an opinion as to whether the City Council may dictate standards and materials used for outdoor bike facilities. 5.

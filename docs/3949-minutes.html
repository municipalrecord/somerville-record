City Council Organizational Meeting Meeting Minutes January 2, 2026 Councilor Davis was nominated by Councilor Mbah, the nomination was seconded by Councilor Ewen-Campen. There being no further nominations, Councilor Ewen-Campen moved to close nominations, the motion was seconded by Councilor Link and approved on a voice vote. Councilor Davis was elected as President of the City Council for 2026. City Clerk Kimberly Wells administered the oath of office to Councilor Davis, who then assumed the chair. APPROVED RESULT: City Councilor At Large Link, City Councilor At Large Mbah, City Councilor At Large Strezo, City Councilor At Large Wheeler, Ward One City Councilor McLaughlin, Ward Three City Councilor Ewen-Campen, Ward Four City Councilor Clingan, Ward Five City Councilor Sait, Ward Six City Councilor Davis, and Ward Seven City Councilor Hardt AYE: Ward Two City Councilor Scott ABSENT: 3.2. Election of a Vice President for 2026. Election (ID # 26-0004) MOTION: That Councilor Mbah be elected Vice President of this City Council. Councilor Mbah was nominated by Councilor McLaughlin, the nomination was seconded by Councilor Clingan. There being no further nominations, Councilor Ewen-Campen moved to close nominations, the motion was seconded by Councilor Link and approved on a voice vote. Councilor Mbah was unanimously elected as President of the City Council for 2026. City Clerk Kimberly Wells administered the oath of office to Councilor Mbah. APPROVED RESULT: City Councilor At Large Link, City Councilor At Large Mbah, City Councilor At Large Strezo, City Councilor At Large Wheeler, Ward One City Councilor McLaughlin, Ward Two City Councilor Scott, Ward Three City Councilor Ewen-Campen, Ward Four City Councilor Clingan, Ward Five City Councilor Sait, Ward Six City Councilor Davis, and Ward Seven City Councilor Hardt AYE: 4.

City of Somerville, Massachusetts City Council City Council Caucus Meeting Minutes 6:30 PM Tuesday, November 21, 2023 NOTICE: This is NOT the official version of the City Council's minutes. While reasonable efforts have been made to assure the accuracy of the data provided, do not rely on this information without first checking with the City Clerk. The meeting was called to order at 6:34 PM by the City Clerk. 1. CALL TO ORDER Present: Willie Burnley Jr. Wilfred N. Mbah Kristen Strezo Jake Wilson Matthew McLaughlin Jefferson Thomas (J.T.) Scott Ben Ewen-Campen Jesse Clingan Naima Sait Lance L. Davis Judy Pineda Neufeld 1.1. Call of the Roll. Roll Call (ID # 23-1712) PLACED ON FILE RESULT: 2. ELECTION OF A PRESIDENT FOR 2024 2.1. Election of a President of the 2024 City Council (nonbinding vote). Election (ID # 23-1713) Councilor Wilson nominated Councilor Ewen-Campen for President. Councilor Davis seconded the motion. There being no other nominations, Councilor Scott moved to close nominations. The motion was seconded by Councilor Pineda Neufeld. That motion was approved on a Roll Call vote of 11 in favor (Burnley, Mbah, Strezo, Wilson, McLaughlin, Scott, Ewen-Campen, Clingan, Sait, Davis, Pineda Neufeld), 0 opposed, 0 absent. Councilor Ewen-Campen was elected as President, on a unanimous non-binding Roll Call vote of 11 in favor (Burnley, Mbah, Strezo, Wilson, McLaughlin, Scott, Ewen-Campen, Clingan, Sait, Davis, Pineda Neufeld), 0 opposed, 0 absent. City Council Caucus Meeting Minutes November 21, 2023 PLACED ON FILE RESULT: 3. ELECTION OF A VICE PRESIDENT FOR 2024 3.1. Election of a Vice President of the 2024 City Council (nonbinding vote). Election (ID # 23-1714) Councilor McLaughlin nominated Councilor Pineda Neufeld for Vice President. Councilor Clingan seconded the motion. There being no other nominations, Councilor Davis moved to close nominations. The motion was seconded by Councilor Wilson. That motion was approved on a Roll Call vote of 10 in favor (Mbah, Strezo, Wilson, McLaughlin, Scott, Ewen-Campen, Clingan, Sait, Davis, Pineda Neufeld), 0 opposed, 1 present (Burnley). Councilor Pineda Neufeld was elected as Vice President, on a non-binding Roll Call vote of 8 in favor (Strezo, Wilson, McLaughlin, Ewen-Campen, Clingan, Sait, Davis, Pineda Neufeld), 0 opposed, 3 present (Burnley, Mbah, Scott). PLACED ON FILE RESULT: 4. ADJOURNMENT Councilor Strezo moved to adjourn. The motion was seconded by Councilor Pineda Neufeld. The motion was approved on a Roll Call vote of 9 in favor (Burnley, Mbah, Strezo, Wilson, Scott, Ewen-Campen, Sait, Davis, Pineda Neufeld), 0 opposed, 2 present (McLaughlin, Clingan) The meeting was Adjourned at 6:40 PM.

File 190747

That the Commissioner of Public Works and Director of Traffic and Parking arrange a meeting with Comcast and the Ward 6 Alderman to consider closing the driveway onto Wallace Street at Holland Street, raising the curb, and planting trees along the sidewalk, to to slow traffic, improve air quality, and lower the noise created by Orleans customers at night.
